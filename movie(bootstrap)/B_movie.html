"The First Slam Dunk" is a sports animation film written and directed by the original manga creator, Takehiko Inoue. It depicts the legendary match against Sannoh Industrial High School from the original series. Shifting the focus from Hanamichi Sakuragi to Ryota Miyagi, the story centers on Ryota’s past and personal growth. By blending modern 3D CG technology with a unique art style that brings raw pen strokes to life, the film delivers both high-energy basketball action and deep emotional resonance. It gained massive popularity among fans in their 30s and 40s, setting record-breaking box office milestones for Japanese animation.
